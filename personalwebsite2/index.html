POSSE Foundation Scholarship

POSSE is found in major cities across the US. Each city has different partner schools, Texas A&M is a partner school for Houston and Atlanta. Only four students from each school in each city can be nominated. These students then have to undergo three separate rounds of interviews. The first and last interview are group interviews, while the second is a solo. In the group interviews, the students are carefully watched, rated on their ability to support, work and lead others. By the third interview, there is only about 25 students left, and only 10 are chosen. These 10 students then become each others support system throughout college and are awarded full tuition scholarships. These students are also expected to attend weekly meetings, workshops and check ins with their mentor. POSSE students are expected to thrive and become leaders in their respective community.
